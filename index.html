The Genesis Center
An all-expenses-paid co-learning residency dedicated to the accelerated advancement of the next generation of Bitcoin creators.

About

The Genesis Center is a self-directed, community-driven educational retreat for programmers in New York City.

Immerse Yourself

Spend 3 months focusing on learning as much as you can. We give you all the resources to surround yourself with the most interesting people and opportunties in Bitcoin.

Build a Network

You will be sharing your days with some of the brightest minds from around the world. We also provide you an industry leading expert as a mentor and connect you to the best minds in the field.

Contribute

Help make Bitcoin better by contributing to open source projects. This helps residents establish themselves and helps the ecosystem.

Details

The residency is a three month program hosted in Manhattan, New York City. Attendees will be expected to commit to coming into the space every work day. We will provide monthly stipends as well as cover travel and lodging to ensure this opportunity is available to all candidates regardless of financial circumstance.

There are no instructors or daily lectures at the Genesis Center. The learners are responsible for their own education. Self-direction does not imply a lack of structure or isolation. Whether participants are working together or in parallel, they will be building towards a common goal to improve Bitcoin and coach each other along with their assigned mentors.


Who we're looking for

Ideal applicants are experienced coders, designers and product managers interested in improving working on Bitcoin and related technologies full-time. Bitcoin needs of a diverse set of skills and talents to succeed including Bitcoin protocol devs, Lightning Network app engineers, UX designers and product managers. Departing from a focus on specific disciplines (i.e. particular technologies or skills) will allow for more vibrant and dynamic cohorts where creativity and cross-collaboration solidify life-long bonds.

The Genesis Center is founded on the principle of recruiting a diverse pool of residents from across the globe. This diversity is important on both sides: it allows us to share our network and resources and provides us with important contrasting perspectives and different skills. We will support the securing of visas and provide the financial assistance to ensure we are getting the best talent on the planet that will inspire residents, mentors and hiring partners alike.


How this is funded

The Genesis Center has an integrated recruiting agency. We help companies of all sizes hire our alumni. We charge companies 25% of a candidate’s first year salary, excluding bonuses, with a 90-day guarantee. If it doesn’t work out during that time, we’ll cancel the invoice for the full amount. Residents don’t ever pay us anything to help them find a role or to attend. If you care about building productive, diverse teams, get in touch.

Our Volunteer Mentors

Each resident is matched with a mentor and they meet for one hour every week for the duration of the program. Mentors are involved from the very beginniner and select their mentees as a criteria for admission.
